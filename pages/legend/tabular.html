Tabular

Description:

These elements are used for creating and structuring tables in HTML. They allow for the organization of data into rows and columns, making it easier to present complex information in a structured format.

Elements:
  • <col>
  • <table>
  • <colgroup>
  • <tr>
  • <caption>
  • <td>
  • <th>
  • <thead>
  • <tbody>
  • <tfoot>
